SMS Service Templates — Draft v2

7 services. Each leads with a service-specific action verb (not the flat “تمت معالجة / has been processed”) and carries the real detail the form holds — bank name, amount, requested document, salary month, violation. Three are completions (Salary Transfer, Salary Deduction, Clearance), three are acknowledgements that the request was logged (HR Request, Passport, Resignation), and one is a disciplinary notice (Warning). Arabic-default; Arabic labels taken verbatim from the app catalog where they exist. Sent manually by an admin via the “Notify by SMS” button.

✓ Confirmed: no second SMS — HR Request / Passport / Resignation say “سيتم إبلاغك … / you will be notified”, and that later notice is delivered by phone or in person, so the wording holds. These acknowledgements exist to reassure the employee that his request was received.

Salary Transfer Request طلب تحويل راتب verb: تم اعتماد · approved key: Salary Transfer Request
العربية
عزيزي محمد أحمد، تم اعتماد طلب تحويل راتبك إلى حسابك لدى بنك أبوظبي الأول. سيتم التحويل مع راتب شهر أغسطس 2026. لأي استفسار يرجى مراجعة مكتب الموارد البشرية. إدارة مركز الإصلاح والتأهيل بالوثبة
عزيزي {الاسم}، تم اعتماد طلب تحويل راتبك إلى حسابك لدى {اسم البنك}. سيتم التحويل مع راتب شهر {شهر الصرف}. لأي استفسار يرجى مراجعة مكتب الموارد البشرية. إدارة مركز الإصلاح والتأهيل بالوثبة
English
Dear Mohammed Ahmed, Your salary transfer request to your account at First Abu Dhabi Bank has been approved. The transfer will take effect with the August 2026 salary. For any clarification, please contact the HR office. Al Wathba Rehabilitation Centre
Dear {name}, Your salary transfer request to your account at {bank name} has been approved. The transfer will take effect with the {salary month} salary. For any clarification, please contact the HR office. Al Wathba Rehabilitation Centre
{salary month} rule (definite): finalized on or before the 15th → next month’s salary; after the 15th → the month after. Sample finalized 05/07/2026 (before 15th) → August 2026. Stated definitely (“سيتم / will”), no hedging.
Token contract: the template already contains the word «شهر», so {شهر الصرف} must be filled with the month name + year only (e.g. «أغسطس 2026»), never «شهر أغسطس 2026» — otherwise the output doubles to «شهر شهر».
