“Send SMS” switch on the form preview

A toggle that appears in the preview footer of any form that notifies the employee. On by default — the notification behaves exactly as it does today. Flip it off before pressing Save and this one book is saved without messaging the employee. Nothing is remembered; the next form starts On again.

Mockup — flip the switches, nothing is wired to the app

Where it sits — the Save-Book footer

Left = English (LTR). Right = Arabic (RTL) — the switch, label and hint mirror. The “what gets sent” panel dims when the switch is off so it reads at a glance.

Only shows on the 8 forms that notify

For every other form (General Books, etc.) the row simply isn’t rendered — no empty switch, no clutter.
